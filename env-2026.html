📐 Ground-level Ozone (Secondary Pollutant)

NOx + VOC → chemical reaction → GLO → Short-lived (hours to weeks) → Delhi-NCR worst affected (2025) → 8-hr safe limit: 100 μg/m³ → Worsens asthma, bronchitis

📐 PFAS (Forever Chemicals)

Carbon-fluorine bonds → very strong → don't degrade → Found in cookware, packaging, fabrics → Bioaccumulate in humans → 20 bacteria species discovered that can break down PFAS (2025)

📐 FGD (Flue Gas Desulphurisation)

Removes SO₂ from coal plant exhaust → Scrubbing via alkaline reagent → 2015 policy: mandatory for all coal plants → New study: Recommended only for imported coal / >0.5% sulphur coal

📐 E-flow (Environmental Flow)

📐 Aerosol & Twomey Effect

Tiny solid/liquid particles in atmosphere → Primary (sea spray, dust) + Secondary (sulfates from industry) → Twomey Effect: More aerosols → smaller cloud droplets → brighter clouds → more sunlight reflected → cooling effect → Solar dimming from anthropogenic aerosols identified

GEOGRAPHY & EARTH SCIENCES

[Prelims]
🌊 AMOC (Atlantic Meridional Overturning Circulation)
  • Brings warm water north + cold water south in Atlantic
  • Driving force: Temperature + salinity differences (thermohaline)
  • 12,000-yr reconstruction shows unprecedented modern weakening
  • Cause: Freshwater influx from melting Greenland ice
  • India impact: Disrupts monsoon rainfall patterns
  • AMOC slowdown → Carbon feedback: less ocean CO₂ uptake → more warming
  • AMOC is a climate tipping point (IPCC)
🔬 Bharat Forecast System (BFS)
  • Developed by IITM Pune | World's FIRST indigenously developed high-res system
  • Resolution: 12km → 6km (ahead of US/UK/EU at 9-14km)
  • 64% better accuracy in high-risk zones
  • HPC: Arka (IITM Pune) + Arunika (NCMRWF Delhi)
  • 40 Doppler Weather Radars integrated
  • Forecast in 4-6 hrs (vs 12-14 hrs earlier)
  • Mission Mausam: MoES 2024 | IMD + NCMRWF + IITM
🌋 Pacific Ring of Fire
  • Horseshoe-shaped belt around Pacific Ocean
  • ~75% world's active volcanoes + ~90% earthquakes
  • Kamchatka 8.8 quake (2025) → tsunami triggered
  • Shallow reverse faulting = upthrust of one block over another
  • Convergent boundaries → subduction zones → volcanoes
  • Mariana Trench = deepest ocean trench (in Ring of Fire subduction zone)
🌊 Deep-Sea & Ocean Science
  • Polymetallic Sulphides (PMS): India signed 15-yr exploration contract with ISA
  • Location: Carlsberg Ridge (Arabian Sea) → FIRST licence for PMS here globally
  • PMS = Cu, Zn, Au, Ag from hydrothermal vents on ocean floor
  • Polymetallic Nodules (manganese nodules): Central Indian Ocean Basin
  • ISA = manages seabed ("common heritage of mankind" under UNCLOS)
  • Deep ocean: <200m = photic zone | Average temp 4°C | Extreme pressure
🌡️ Climate Geography Concepts
  • LPA: Long-period average (1971-2020 = 87cm) | Normal = 96-104% | Excess = >110%
  • True Polar Wander (TPW): Dam construction shifted Earth's axis >1m since 1835 → longer days
  • Delamination: Indian plate's dense lower section peels + sinks into mantle → new theory for rapid plate movement
  • Arabian Sea Mini Warm Pool: SST >28.5°C | Apr-May | Self-correcting mechanism for monsoon post-El Niño
  • Thirstwave: ≥3 consecutive days of >90th percentile evaporative demand
  • Cold Pools: Evaporatively cooled downdraft air spreading under precipitating clouds
  • Solar Tornado: Ionized gas/plasma held by magnetic fields at Sun's poles | 130,000 km height | 250,000°C
