Health, AYUSH & Nutrition
8 SCHEMES
e-Rakt Kosh
MoHFW · NHM Developed by C-DAC
  • Centralised Blood Bank Management Information System (BBMIS)
  • Digitises & integrates all blood banks across India on ONE platform
  • 4 modules: Donor · Blood Bank · Patient · Testing
  • Integrated with Rare Donor Registry of India (RDRI)
  • Implemented under National Health Mission (NHM)
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Developed by C-DAC (not NIC, not MoHFW directly). Implemented under NHM. Do NOT confuse developer ≠ implementing ministry.

PMSMA — Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan
MoHFW 2016
  • Free Antenatal Care (ANC) on 9th of every month
  • At government health facilities only
  • Target: Pregnant women in 2nd & 3rd trimester only
  • Private doctors encouraged to volunteer (not mandatory)
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Fixed date = 9th of every month. Target = 2nd & 3rd trimester ONLY (not 1st). Private doctor participation is VOLUNTARY.

ICDS — Integrated Child Development Services
Centrally Sponsored MoWCD 1975
  • One of world's largest child development programmes; launched 1975
  • Target: Children 0–6 yrs, pregnant & lactating mothers, adolescent girls
  • Delivery: through Anganwadi Centres (AWCs)
  • 6 services: Supplementary Nutrition · Pre-school Education · N&H Education · Immunisation · Health Check-ups · Referral
  • Now subsumed under Saksham Anganwadi & POSHAN 2.0
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Immunisation under ICDS is via convergence with NHM/Health Ministry — it is NOT directly implemented by MoWCD. Subsumed under POSHAN 2.0, NOT abolished.

Ayushman Sahakar Scheme
NCDC · Ministry of Cooperation 2020
  • Implementing body: NCDC (National Cooperative Development Corporation) — apex statutory autonomous institution
  • Term loans up to ₹10,000 crore to cooperatives for healthcare
  • 1% interest subvention for women-led cooperatives
  • For: hospitals, health centres, modernisation & expansion
  • Earlier under Ministry of Agriculture; now Ministry of Cooperation (est. 2021)
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Ministry = Cooperation (not Agriculture, not Health). NCDC ≠ NABARD. Interest subvention of 1% is ONLY for women-led cooperatives.

DRAVYA Portal
Ministry of AYUSH · CCRAS
  • Full form: Digitized Retrieval Application for Versatile Yardstick of AYUSH Substances
  • Developed by CCRAS (Central Council for Research in Ayurvedic Sciences)
  • Digital database of aahara (food) & aushadhi (medicinal) substances
  • Integrated with AYUSH Grid
🧠 Memory Hook

DRAVYA = AYUSH substances database. CCRAS makes it, AYUSH Grid integrates it. Remember: DRAVYA = Dravya (substance) in Sanskrit!

NPSN — National Polio Surveillance Network
MoHFW + WHO (Joint) Est. 1997
  • Joint initiative: MoHFW India + WHO
  • Objective: Surveillance of Acute Flaccid Paralysis (AFP)
  • Tracks every AFP case in children <15 years
  • Also covers Vaccine Preventable Diseases (VPDs)
  • Ensures zero missed cases; provides real-time data
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Joint initiative with WHO, not just MoHFW alone. Tracks AFP in children <15 years (not under 5).

Education, Skills & Human Capital
14 SCHEMES
PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)
Central Sector MSDE · NSDC 2015
  • Flagship under Skill India Mission; Implementing agency: NSDC
  • Components: STT · Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL) · Special Projects
  • Training is FREE for candidates (NOT subsidy — just free training)
  • Certification based on NSQF; third-party assessment by SSCs
  • PMKVY 4.0: Industry 4.0 — AI, robotics, coding, drones; aligned with NEP 2020
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Ministry = MSDE, NOT Labour Ministry. Implementing = NSDC. Assessment by Sector Skill Councils (SSCs), not NSDC directly. RPL is for informal workers with existing skills.

PM Internship Scheme (PMIS)
Ministry of Corporate Affairs
  • Ministry: Corporate Affairs (NOT Labour, NOT MSDE — common trap!)
  • Target: 1 crore youth internships over 5 years
  • Eligibility: Age 21–24 yrs; NOT in full-time employment/education
  • Top 500 companies based on CSR expenditure
  • Duration: 12 months | Stipend: ₹5,000/month + one-time ₹6,000
  • Priority: low-income, non-elite educational backgrounds
  • Distance/online learners = ALLOWED to apply
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Ministry = Corporate Affairs NOT MSDE. Age = 21–24 NOT 18–35. Duration = 12 months. Companies selected on CSR spend, not revenue.

PM SHRI Scheme
Centrally Sponsored Ministry of Education 2022
  • Transform existing schools into 'Exemplar Schools' showcasing NEP 2020
  • Period: 2022–23 to 2026–27
  • Covers: KVs, NVs, State/UT govt schools, local body schools
  • Performance tracked via School Quality Assessment Framework (SQAF)
  • Funding: 60:40 General | 90:10 NE/Himalayan | 100% UTs without legislature
🧠 Memory Hook

PM SHRI = Schools for Holistic Rise of India. Launched 2022 = 75th independence year, NEP 2020 implementation.

TALASH Initiative
Ministry of Tribal Affairs · NESTS With UNICEF India
  • Full form: Tribal Aptitude, Life Skills and Self-Esteem Hub
  • Launched by: Min of Tribal Affairs; Implemented by: NESTS
  • Developed with UNICEF India
  • Target: Students of EMRS (Eklavya Model Residential Schools)
  • Focus: Psychometric Assessment + Career Mapping + Life Skills
  • First dedicated national programme for tribal career + psychological development
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Implementing = NESTS (not NCST, not NTA). Collaboration with UNICEF, not World Bank. Target = EMRS students, not all tribal students.

One Nation One Subscription (ONOS)
Central Sector DHE · Ministry of Education
  • Access to 13,000+ scholarly journals; ~30 major international publishers
  • Covers: STEM, medicine, social sciences, finance
  • Budget: ₹6,000 crore for 2025–2027 (3 years)
  • Implemented by: Department of Higher Education (DHE)
  • Democratize access to research across India
⚡ UPSC TRAP

It's Central Sector (not CSS). Ministry = Education → DHE (not UGC, not MeitY). Budget = ₹6,000 crore / 3 years.

SANKALP Scheme
Central Sector MSDE 2018 World Bank Aided
  • Full form: Skill Acquisition and Knowledge Awareness for Livelihood Promotion
  • World Bank-assisted (loan-based) programme
  • Strengthens institutional mechanisms; district-level skill planning
  • Targets: Youth, women, SC/ST, PwD — marginalised sections
  • Strengthens: Sector Skill Councils (SSCs) + State Skill Development Missions (SSDMs)
🧠 Memory Hook

SANKALP = World Bank + MSDE + Skill institutions. Unlike PMKVY (individual skilling), SANKALP = institutional capacity building.

PM-YUVA 3.0
Central Sector Ministry of Education · NBT
  • PM's Young Authors Mentorship Scheme
  • Implementing agency: National Book Trust (NBT)
  • Target: Young authors below 30 years; selection via All India Contest
  • Scholarship: ₹50,000/month × 6 months = ₹3 lakh per author
  • Books published by NBT India
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Implementing = NBT (not Sahitya Akademi). Age = below 30. Scholarship = ₹50,000/month for 6 months.

NCS Portal — National Career Service
Ministry of Labour & Employment 2015
  • Mission Mode Project (e-Governance); replaced National Employment Service (NES)
  • Features: AI-based job matching + LMIS + career counselling
  • Links: Skill India Mission + e-Shram Portal + Digital India
  • Network of Model Career Centres (MCCs)
🧠 Memory Hook

NCS replaced NES (old system). Labour Ministry runs it. Skill India (MSDE) ≠ NCS — NCS is job-matching, not skill training.

Agriculture, Food Security & Rural Dev
10 SCHEMES
PM-POSHAN (Mid Day Meal Upgrade)
Centrally Sponsored Ministry of Education 2021
  • Replaces Mid-Day Meal Scheme (1995); MDM started in 1925 in Madras Municipal Corporation
  • Covers: Classes I–VIII; govt & govt-aided schools; Madrasas (under Samagra Shiksha)
  • Nutrition norms: Primary = 450 kcal + 12g protein | Upper Primary = 700 kcal + 20g protein
  • Funding: 60:40 | 90:10 NE/Himalayan | 100% UTs
  • New features: Poshan Vatika (Nutrition Gardens), Tithi Bhojan, FPO involvement
  • Legal backing: NFSA 2013
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Ministry = Education (NOT WCD, NOT Agriculture). Coverage = Classes I–VIII only. MDM History: Madras Municipal Corp 1925 → National scheme 1995 → PM-POSHAN 2021.

PMGKAY — Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana
Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food & PD Mar 2020
  • Launched: March 2020 as COVID relief; 7 phases till Dec 2022
  • From Jan 2023: merged into NFSA — free 5 kg foodgrain/person/month
  • NFSA beneficiaries NO LONGER pay ₹2/₹3 per kg
  • Beneficiaries: AAY + Priority Households (PHH) under NFSA 2013
  • Fully funded by Central Government
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Post Jan 2023, PMGKAY is subsumed into NFSA — not a separate scheme. Ministry = Consumer Affairs, Food & PD (not Agriculture, not Rural Dev).

NBS — Nutrient Based Subsidy Scheme
Ministry of Chemicals & Fertilizers 2010
  • Subsidy fixed per kg of N, P, K, Sulphur — NOT on final fertilizer product
  • Applies to P&K fertilizers (DAP, MOP, NPK complex)
  • UREA IS NOT COVERED under NBS
  • Subsidy given to manufacturers/importers, NOT directly to farmers
  • Companies free to set retail prices
  • Revised: Usually biannually (Rabi & Kharif seasons)
⚡ UPSC TRAP

UREA IS EXCLUDED from NBS. Subsidy is on nutrient content, NOT product. Subsidy goes to manufacturers NOT farmers. Ministry = Chemicals & Fertilizers NOT Agriculture.

PM Dhan-Dhaanya Krishi Yojana (PMDDKY)
Centrally Sponsored Ministry of Agriculture Budget 2025–26
  • Targets 100 districts with low productivity across 29 States/UTs
  • Duration: 6 years — 2025–26 to 2030–31
  • Selection criteria: low productivity + low irrigation + low credit penetration
  • Convergence model: PMKSY + PMFBY + Soil Health Card + e-NAM + KCC
  • Focus: 'Aspirational Agriculture Districts' (like Aspirational District Programme)
🧠 Memory Hook

PMDDKY = Aspirational Agriculture Districts (100 districts, 6 years). It INTEGRATES existing schemes — does NOT create new subsidies.

e-NAM — National Agriculture Market
Ministry of Agriculture · SFAC 2016
  • Pan-India electronic trading portal; implemented by SFAC
  • Networks APMC mandis for unified national market
  • Started: 21 mandis (2016) → expanded to 1000+ mandis
  • Covers ~200+ commodities
  • Objective: 'One Nation, One Market' for agri-produce
  • Features: Warehouse-based trading + e-NWR integration + FPO module + Logistics
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Implemented by SFAC (Small Farmers Agribusiness Consortium), NOT NABARD. Integration with AgriStack/digital agriculture ecosystem.

FPOs — Formation of 10,000 FPOs
Central Sector Ministry of Agriculture Till 2027–28
  • Implementing: SFAC + NABARD + NCDC (3 agencies)
  • Financial support: ₹15 lakh/FPO for 3 years
  • Minimum members: 300 (plain areas) | 100 (hilly/tribal)
  • Principle: One member – One vote; members = shareholders
  • Can be: Producer Company (Companies Act 2013) / Cooperative / other legal form
  • Focus: Aspirational districts + tribal areas + women farmers
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Three implementing agencies: SFAC + NABARD + NCDC. Min members: 300 plains, 100 hills. Support = ₹15 lakh for 3 years (not per year).

Social Welfare, Inclusion & Urban Livelihoods
11 SCHEMES
PM Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY)
Central Sector Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas 2016
  • Clean cooking fuel (LPG) to poor households; beneficiaries: adult women 18+
  • Initially identified using SECC 2011 data
  • Financial assistance: ₹1,600/connection (covers security deposit + pressure regulator)
  • Optional loan for stove & first refill
  • PMUY 2.0 (Aug 2021): Free first refill; no address proof for migrants; self-declaration
  • LPG coverage: ~55% (2014) → ~99% now
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Ministry = Petroleum & Natural Gas (NOT Rural Dev, NOT WCD). ₹1,600 covers ONLY security deposit, NOT stove. Stove is optional LOAN, not grant.

PM SVANidhi Scheme
Central Sector MoHUA
  • Full form: Pradhan Mantri Street Vendor's AtmaNirbhar Nidhi
  • Ministry: MoHUA (Housing & Urban Affairs)
  • Provides affordable working capital loans to street vendors (post-COVID)
  • Formalise informal vendors; integrate into digital + institutional credit system
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Ministry = MoHUA (urban, NOT rural, NOT labour). It's for street vendors only. Working capital loans, NOT grants.

PM RAHAT Scheme
Ministry of Road Transport & Highways
  • Full: PM Road Accident Victims' Hospitalisation & Assured Treatment
  • Cashless treatment up to ₹1.5 lakh for accident victims
  • Reimbursements via Motor Vehicle Accident Fund (MVAF)
  • Integrates: eDAR (Electronic Detailed Accident Report, used by police) with TMS 2.0 (Transaction Mgmt System, used by hospitals)
🧠 Memory Hook

RAHAT = Road + ₹1.5 lakh + eDAR (police) + TMS 2.0 (hospital). MVAF is the fund, not a separate scheme.

Sugamya Bharat App
DEPwD · MoSJE
  • Launched by: DEPwD (Dept of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities)
  • Ministry: MoSJE (Social Justice & Empowerment)
  • Mobile platform for PwDs to report accessibility issues
  • Under: Sugamya Bharat Abhiyan (Accessible India Campaign)
  • Legal: Rights of PwD Act 2016 + UN CRPD
  • 3 pillars: Built Environment · Transport · ICT Accessibility
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Under Accessible India Campaign (not Digital India). Ministry = Social Justice & Empowerment, NOT Health. UNCRPD linkage frequently tested.

Vibrant Villages Programme-II (VVP-II)
Central Sector — 100% funded Ministry of Home Affairs 2024–2029
  • Ministry: Home Affairs (NOT Rural Dev, NOT Panchayati Raj)
  • 100% centrally funded — 5-year programme (2024–25 to 2028–29)
  • Targets border villages in Himalayan/NE states — strategic & developmental
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Ministry = Home Affairs (strategic/border context). 100% Centre funded — unique! Most CSS schemes share with states.

NAMASTE Scheme
MoSJE + MoHUA
  • Full form: National Action for Mechanised Sanitation Ecosystem
  • Joint: MoSJE + MoHUA
  • Implements via: NSKFDC (National Safai Karamcharis Finance & Development Corp)
  • Waste Picker Enumeration App is part of this scheme
  • Goal: Eliminate unsafe sanitation practices; provide machinery & safety gear
🧠 Memory Hook

NAMASTE = two ministries (MoSJE + MoHUA). NSKFDC implements. Waste Picker App is a sub-tool of NAMASTE.

Economy, Industry, MSME & Employment
14 SCHEMES
PM Mudra Yojana
Central Sector Dept of Financial Services · MoF 2015
  • MUDRA: Micro Units Development & Refinance Agency — set up as NBFC under SIDBI
  • Ministry: Department of Financial Services, MoFinance
  • Type: Refinance + Credit Guarantee (NOT direct lending, NOT subsidy)
  • Max loan: ₹10 lakh; generally collateral-free
  • Covers: Non-Farm sector ONLY
  • Shishu ≤₹50K | Kishor ₹50K–₹5L | Tarun ₹5L–₹10L
  • Mudra Card = RuPay debit card for borrowers
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Ministry = Finance (NOT MSME, NOT Rural Dev). MUDRA is NBFC under SIDBI. Covers NON-FARM only. It is NOT a subsidy scheme. Collateral-free = generally (not always).

Startup India
DPIIT · Ministry of Commerce 2016
  • Ministry: Commerce & Industry → DPIIT
  • Startup definition: Age ≤10 years, Turnover ≤₹100 crore
  • DPIIT recognition mandatory to avail all benefits
  • FFS: ₹10,000 crore corpus; managed by SIDBI; invests via AIFs (SEBI registered)
  • SISFS: Grant ≤₹20 lakh (PoC) | Debt ≤₹50 lakh (market entry)
  • CGSS: Up to ₹20 crore guarantee; 85% coverage for loans ≤₹10 crore
  • Tax holiday: 3 consecutive years under Section 80-IAC
  • Angel tax exemption for DPIIT-recognised startups
  • Exit under IBC: 90 days
⚡ UPSC TRAP

FFS managed by SIDBI (not DPIIT). FFS invests in AIFs, not startups directly. Tax holiday = Section 80-IAC (not 80C). Age limit = 10 years from incorporation.

Jan Suraksha Schemes — APY / PMJJBY / PMSBY
Ministry of Finance 2015
  • APY (Atal Pension Yojana): Entry age 18–40; pension from age 60; guaranteed ₹1K–₹5K/month; regulator = PFRDA
  • APY: Govt co-contributed 50% OR ₹1,000/yr (whichever lower) — only for non-income-taxpayers
  • PMJJBY: Death (any reason); age 18–50; 1-year renewable
  • PMSBY: Death/disability due to accident only; age 18–70; 1-year renewable
⚡ UPSC TRAP

APY regulator = PFRDA. PMJJBY max age = 50; PMSBY max = 70. PMSBY = accident ONLY. APY co-contribution: only non-income-taxpayers NOT covered by other social security.

PM E-DRIVE Scheme
Central Sector Ministry of Heavy Industry
  • Full: PM Electric Drive Revolution in Innovative Vehicle Enhancement
  • Ministry: Heavy Industry (NOT MoRTH, NOT MSME)
  • Successor/continuation of FAME India Scheme Phase II
  • Demand incentives: e-2W, e-3W, e-ambulances, e-trucks, e-buses
  • EV charging infra on highways & urban areas
  • Complements: PLI Scheme for ACC Batteries
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Ministry = Heavy Industry. It succeeds FAME II. Do not confuse with MoRTH (roads). Complements ACC battery PLI = chemistry cell batteries.

Gold Monetisation Scheme
Ministry of Finance · RBI
  • Mobilize idle gold; reduce gold imports
  • Eligibility: Resident Indians ONLY (HUFs, firms, trusts, companies, Govt, charities)
  • Minimum deposit: 10 grams | Maximum: No limit
  • Accepted: gold bars, coins, jewellery (excluding stones/metals)
  • Active deposit now: Short-Term Bank Deposit (STBD) → 1–3 years ONLY
  • MTGD & LTGD discontinued from March 26, 2025
  • Interest: always paid in INR; Principal: gold OR INR (choice at deposit time)
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Interest = always in INR (not gold). MTGD/LTGD discontinued March 2025. Only STBD (1–3 yr) active now. NRIs = NOT eligible.

GeM — Govt e-Marketplace
Ministry of Commerce · DPIIT 2016
  • Online platform for public procurement; developed as Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV)
  • Procurement modes: Direct Purchase | L1 | Bidding | Reverse Auction
  • Integrated: Aadhaar + PAN + MCA21 + GSTN
  • Aabhar Online Store: promotes ODOP + GI items; sourced from CCIE / KVIC / Handicraft emporiums
🧠 Memory Hook

GeM = SPV (not a ministry directly). Reverse auction = price comes DOWN. Aabhar = artisans + heritage = CCIE + KVIC.

Digital Governance, Technology & Innovation
16 SCHEMES
Jal Jeevan Mission
Ministry of Jal Shakti · DDWS 2019
  • Provide FHTC (Functional Household Tap Connection) to every rural household
  • Service norm: 55 LPCD (litres per capita per day) as per BIS standards
  • Funding: 90:10 Himalayan/NE | 50:50 others | 100% UTs w/o legislature
  • Water Quality: Field Test Kits (FTKs); at least 5 persons/village trained (esp. women)
  • IoT-based monitoring; real-time dashboard on JJM portal
  • VWSC (Village Water & Sanitation Committee) plays key role
  • Special focus: Arsenic/Fluoride-affected areas, SC/ST habitations, Aspirational districts
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Implemented by DDWS (Dept of Drinking Water & Sanitation) under Jal Shakti. Norm = 55 LPCD (not 40, not 100). Women trained = 5/village minimum. AMRUT ≠ JJM (AMRUT = urban, JJM = rural).

SVAMITVA Scheme
Central Sector Ministry of Panchayati Raj
  • Legal ownership of rural RESIDENTIAL land via drone-based surveys
  • Ministry: Panchayati Raj
  • Technical support: Survey of India + NICSI
  • Provides property rights card to households (not just record)
  • Enables use of property as financial asset (loan collateral)
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Covers residential land (not agricultural). Ministry = Panchayati Raj (not Rural Dev). Survey done by Survey of India using drones (not Revenue Dept).

Green Credit Programme
MoEFCC · ICFRE Budget 2023–24
  • Tradable units for voluntary environmental actions
  • Legal backing: Environment (Protection) Act, 1986; notified via Green Credit Rules, 2023
  • Administrator: ICFRE (Indian Council of Forestry Research & Education) under MoEFCC
  • Different from Carbon Credits: broader scope (not just carbon)
  • Voluntary participation; linked to LiFE Mission ('pro-planet people' behaviour)
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Administrator = ICFRE (NOT MoEFCC directly, NOT BEE). Green Credits ≠ Carbon Credits. Legal basis = EP Act 1986. Participation is VOLUNTARY.

AMRUT & AMRUT 2.0
Centrally Sponsored MoHUA
  • Coverage: Initially 500 cities (population ≥1 lakh + special category towns)
  • 5 sectors: Water supply · Sewerage · Storm water drainage · Urban transport · Green spaces
  • Funding: 90:10 NE/Himalayan | 50:50 others
  • Planning: State Annual Action Plans (SAAPs) — bottom-up approach
  • AMRUT 2.0: Focus = 'Water Secure Cities'; circular water economy; all statutory towns
  • AMRUT 2.0 converges with Jal Jeevan Mission (Urban)
⚡ UPSC TRAP

AMRUT = urban. JJM = rural. AMRUT 2.0 includes ALL statutory towns (not just 500). Planning = SAAPs (state-level, bottom-up, NOT centrally directed).

ONDC — Open Network for Digital Commerce
DPIIT · Ministry of Commerce
  • Based on open network protocols (NOT a platform)
  • NOT an e-commerce platform like Amazon/Flipkart
  • Enables interoperability between buyers, sellers, logistics providers, payment gateways
  • Enables platform-agnostic transactions
⚡ UPSC TRAP

ONDC is a network/protocol, NOT a marketplace/platform. It does NOT sell products. Competitors can coexist on the same network. Ministry = DPIIT/Commerce (not MeitY).

SIGHT Scheme — Green Hydrogen
MNRE · SECI + IREDA
  • Full: Strategic Interventions for Green Hydrogen Transition
  • Major financial component of National Green Hydrogen Mission (NGHM)
  • Two components: (1) Incentives for Green Hydrogen production (₹/kg) | (2) Electrolyser manufacturing support
  • Implementing: SECI + IREDA
  • Allocation via competitive bidding
  • Target sectors: fertilizer, refinery, steel, heavy transport, shipping, export
⚡ UPSC TRAP

SIGHT is under NGHM, not a standalone mission. Two distinct components: production incentive AND electrolyser manufacturing. Implementing = SECI + IREDA (not NTPC, not MoPNG).

Parvatmala Pariyojana
MoRTH · NHLML Budget 2022–23
  • Full: National Ropeways Development Programme
  • Ministry: MoRTH; Implementing: NHLML (National Highways Logistics Management Limited)
  • Model: PPP + Hybrid Annuity Model (HAM)
  • Purpose: last-mile connectivity in hilly areas; eco-friendly transport
  • Key projects: Kedarnath, Hemkund Sahib, Varanasi (urban)
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Implementing body = NHLML (NOT NHIDCL, not NHAI). Ministry = MoRTH (roads, not tourism). PPP via HAM model.

Sanchar Saathi App
DoT · Ministry of Communications
  • Ministry: Communications → DoT
  • TAFCOP: Telecom Analytics for Fraud Mgmt — check/report SIMs in your name
  • CEIR: Central Equipment Identity Register — block lost/stolen phones by IMEI
  • KYM: Know Your Mobile — verify phone genuineness before purchase
🧠 Memory Hook

Sanchar Saathi = 3 modules: TAFCOP (SIM fraud) + CEIR (stolen phones) + KYM (fake phones). All consumer protection. DoT = Ministry of Communications.

DHRUVA — Digital Address System
Dept of Posts · Ministry of Communications
  • Full: Digital Hub for Reference and Unique Virtual Address
  • National digital address system: standardised, interoperable, consent-based
  • Unique Virtual Address (UVA) = email-style ID for person/household/institution
  • Geocoded: linked to lat-long coordinates
  • Builds on DIGIPIN — geo-coded 4m×4m grid addressing system
  • Nature: Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI)
⚡ UPSC TRAP

DHRUVA ≠ DIGIPIN. DHRUVA = platform layer; DIGIPIN = foundational grid. Ministry = Communications/Posts (not MeitY, not UIDAI).

National Cooperative Policy 2025
Ministry of Cooperation (est. 2021)
  • Replaces National Policy on Cooperatives (2002)
  • Ministry of Cooperation established in 2021 (new ministry)
  • Vision: 'Sahkar se Samriddhi' (Prosperity through cooperation)
  • Digitisation of PACS (Primary Agricultural Credit Societies)
  • Amendments to Multi-State Cooperative Societies Act
  • Bharat Taxi: India's first cooperative-led ride-hailing platform under this ministry
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Ministry of Cooperation = new ministry est. 2021. Replaces 2002 policy. PACS digitisation + MSCS Act amendments. 'Sahkar se Samriddhi' = slogan.

C-Flood Portal
CWC · Ministry of Jal Shakti
  • Computational Flood Forecasting platform
  • Launched by: Central Water Commission (CWC)
  • Forecasts flood inundation up to village level
  • Lead time: up to 48 hours (2 days) advance forecast
  • Provides Flood Inundation Maps (area to be submerged)
  • Moves from station-based to area-based (inundation) forecasts
⚡ UPSC TRAP

Launched by CWC (not NDMA, not IMD). Forecast = village level. Lead time = 48 hours. Inundation maps = area flooded (not just river level).
