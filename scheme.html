Health, AYUSH & Nutrition
9 SCHEMES
Swasth Nari Sashakt Parivar Abhiyan
MoHFW + MoWCD
  • Improve health & nutrition of women & children
  • Target: Pregnant women, lactating mothers, adolescent girls, children <5
  • Convergence with POSHAN Abhiyaan, Ayushman Bharat, JSY
  • Involves ASHA & Anganwadi workers; aligned with Rashtriya Poshan Maah (Sep)
DRAVYA Portal
Ministry of AYUSH \u00b7 CCRAS
  • Digitized Retrieval Application for Versatile Yardstick of AYUSH Substances
  • Comprehensive digital database of aahara (food) & aushadhi (medicinal) substances
  • Integrated with AYUSH Grid; promotes global standardisation
e-Rakt Kosh
MoHFW \u00b7 NHMC-DAC
  • Centralised Blood Bank Management Information System (BBMIS)
  • Digitises & integrates blood banks across India on a single platform
  • 4 modules: Donor, Blood Bank, Patient, Testing
  • Integrated with Rare Donor Registry of India (RDRI)
Ayush Nivesh Saarthi Portal
Ministry of AYUSH \u00b7 Invest India
  • Digital investment facilitation portal for AYUSH sector
  • Single-window access; promotes domestic & foreign investment
  • Enhances Ease of Doing Business (EoDB) in traditional medicine
PM Surakshit Matritva Abhiyan (PMSMA)
MoHFW2016
  • Free Antenatal Care (ANC) on 9th of every month at govt health facilities
  • Target: Pregnant women in 2nd & 3rd trimester
  • Private doctors encouraged to volunteer
National Polio Surveillance Network (NPSN)
MoHFW + WHOEst. 1997
  • Surveillance of Acute Flaccid Paralysis (AFP)
  • Tracks every AFP case in children <15 years
  • Ensures zero missed cases; provides real-time data
Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS)
MoWCD \u00b7 CSS1975
  • Holistic development: children 0\u20136 yrs, pregnant & lactating women
  • 6 services via Anganwadi Centres: Supplementary Nutrition, Pre-school Education, Nutrition & Health Education, Immunisation, Health Check-ups, Referral
  • Now subsumed under Saksham Anganwadi & POSHAN 2.0
Ayushman Sahakar Scheme
NCDC \u00b7 Ministry of Cooperation2020
  • Term loans up to \u20b910,000 crore to cooperatives for healthcare
  • 1% interest subvention for women-led cooperatives
  • NCDC = apex level statutory autonomous institution

Education, Skills & Human Capital
16 SCHEMES
TALASH Initiative
Ministry of Tribal Affairs \u00b7 NESTS
  • Tribal Aptitude, Life Skills & Self-Esteem Hub
  • Target: EMRS students (tribal adolescents); developed with UNICEF India
  • Psychometric Assessment + Career Mapping + Life Skills Training
  • First dedicated national-level programme for tribal career + psychological development
National Career Service (NCS) Portal
Ministry of Labour & Employment2015
  • Mission Mode Project; replaced National Employment Service (NES)
  • AI-based job matching; career counselling; Labour Market Info System (LMIS)
  • Linked with Skill India Mission, e-Shram & Digital India
PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY)
MSDE \u00b7 NSDC2015
  • Flagship scheme under Skill India Mission
  • Components: Short-Term Training (STT), Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL), Special Projects
  • PMKVY 4.0: Focus on Industry 4.0 skills \u2014 AI, robotics, coding, drones
  • Based on NSQF; Third-party assessment by Sector Skill Councils (SSCs)
  • Training is free of cost for candidates
PM Internship Scheme (PMIS)
Ministry of Corporate Affairs
  • Target: 1 crore youth internships over 5 years
  • Age: 21\u201324 years; top 500 companies (based on CSR expenditure)
  • Duration: 12 months; Stipend: \u20b95,000/month + one-time \u20b96,000
  • Priority to low-income, non-elite educational background candidates
NAVYA Initiative
MoWCD + MSDE
  • Nurturing Aspirations through Vocational Training for Young Adolescent Girls
  • Target: Girls aged 16\u201318 yrs who completed at least Class 10
  • Focus on non-traditional sectors: electronics, automotive, renewable energy
  • Training via PMKKs; aligned with NSQF
PM SHRI Scheme
Ministry of Education2022
  • Develops model schools showcasing NEP 2020; period 2022\u201327
  • Transform existing schools into 'exemplar schools'
  • Funding: 60:40 (General), 90:10 (NE/Himalayan), 100% (UTs)
  • Performance tracked via School Quality Assessment Framework (SQAF)
SANKALP Scheme
MSDE \u00b7 World Bank2018
  • Skill Acquisition & Knowledge Awareness for Livelihood Promotion
  • World Bank-assisted; strengthens institutional mechanisms for skill development
  • District-level skill planning; strengthens SSCs & SSDMs
Unnat Bharat Abhiyan (UBA)
Ministry of Education \u00b7 IIT Delhi
  • Connect HEIs with rural India for sustainable development
  • Each HEI adopts at least 5 villages
  • UBA 2.0: cluster of villages approach; outcome-based interventions
One Nation One Subscription (ONOS)
DHE \u00b7 Ministry of Education \u00b7 Central Sector
  • Nationwide access to 13,000+ scholarly journals (~30 major publishers)
  • Covers STEM, medicine, social sciences, finance
  • Budget: \u20b96,000 crore for 2025\u20132027
PM-YUVA 3.0
Ministry of Education \u00b7 NBT
  • Young Authors Mentorship Scheme; target: authors below 30 years
  • Scholarship: \u20b950,000/month \u00d7 6 months = \u20b93 lakh per author
  • Books published by National Book Trust
CBHRD Scheme
CSIR \u00b7 Ministry of S&T
  • Capacity Building & Human Resource Development; Umbrella Scheme
  • Doctoral & postdoctoral fellowships; Shanti Swarup Bhatnagar Fellowship
  • Extramural Research Scheme (ERS); Emeritus Scientist Scheme
Institutes of Eminence (IoE)
MoE \u00b7 UGC2017
  • Project Indian institutions into top global rankings
  • Selected by Empowered Expert Committee (EEC); approved by MoE
  • Declared as 'Institutions of Eminence Deemed to be Universities' by UGC
