1.2 PESA Act, 1996 30th Anniversary

  • Full form: Panchayats (Extension to Scheduled Areas) Act
  • Constitutional basis: Article 243M(4)(b) — extends Part IX (Panchayati Raj) to Fifth Schedule Areas
  • States covered: 10 states — AP, Chhattisgarh, Gujarat, HP, Jharkhand, MP, Maharashtra, Odisha, Rajasthan, Telangana
  • Status: 9/10 states framed PESA rules; Odisha has only draft rules
  • ST seats: Minimum 50% seats + all Chairperson posts reserved for STs
Gram Sabha Powers: Control over intoxicants, minor forest produce ownership, land alienation prevention, money lending to STs, village markets
Mandatory Consultation: Land acquisition, minor water bodies, mining leases, mineral auction concessions

1.6 POCSO Act, 2012 (Amended 2019) Child Protection

  • Protects children (<18 yrs) from sexual assault, harassment, pornography
  • Gender-neutral — applies to any gender of child or offender
  • Storage of child porn: Fine ₹5,000; repeat ₹10,000
  • Reporting: Must report to Special Juvenile Police Unit or local police
  • Police must report to: Child Welfare Committee & Special Court within 24 hrs
  • Failure to report: Up to 6 months imprisonment
  • Special Courts: In-camera trials within 1 year
  • Monitoring: NCPCR + SCPCR
  • SC suggestion: "Romeo-Juliet clause" for consensual adolescent relationships
Australia first country to ban social media for children <16 (Online Safety Amendment Act 2024)

4Environment

4.1 Aravallis SC Judgment

  • One of world's oldest mountain ranges (Pre-Cambrian); predates Himalayas
  • Stretches SW from Delhi through Haryana, Rajasthan → Gujarat
  • Highest peak: Guru Shikhar (Mt. Abu)
  • Rivers: Luni, Sabarmati (west); Banas (east); Sahibi (north)
  • SC Definition: "Aravalli Hill" = landform with ≥100m elevation; "Range" = 2+ hills within 500m proximity
  • Initiatives: Matri Van (750-acre urban forest Gurugram), Aravalli Green Wall Project (5km buffer in 4 states)

4.2 SWM Rules, 2026 Supersedes 2016 Rules

  • Under Environment (Protection) Act, 1986; effective April 1, 2026
  • Integrates Circular Economy and Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR)
  • 4-stream segregation: Wet, Dry, Sanitary, Special Care Waste
  • RDF (Refuse Derived Fuel) use mandatory for cement plants & waste-to-energy plants
  • Annual landfill audits; biomining of legacy waste dumpsites
  • Environmental Compensation (EC) on Polluter Pays principle
India SWM Status: 55-60% biodegradable | 15-20% recyclable | 15-25% hazardous/non-recyclable. Only 60-70% waste collected; 15-20% processed. 60% from urban areas

4.3 Groundwater Report 2025

  • India = world's largest groundwater consumer (~25% of global extraction)
  • Groundwater: 64% irrigation, 85% rural drinking water, ~50% urban water
  • 87% extracted for agriculture, ~11% domestic
  • Stage of Extraction (SoE) = 60.63% (marginally increased)
  • Contaminants: Nitrate (Rajasthan, TN, MH), Fluoride (Haryana, Karnataka), Arsenic (Ganga-Brahmaputra floodplains), Uranium (Rajasthan, Punjab)
